WORK. LIFE. BALANCE

OnCloudX is a quality of life app that shows you your dream place to live. Using our quality of life survey index, we can determine the city that best matches your personality and goals.

The goal of OnCloudX is to provide the most accurate information for people looking to take the next step in life. OnCloudX takes the guesswork out of moving and gives you the best place that best suits your lifestyle choices. Every choice has an impact on your socioeconomic opportunities, and OnCloudX will open the doors for a new life.

For marketers, our proprietary personality quiz is a useful tool to gather information on target audiences. Our quiz can be tailored specifically to your needs to help you create personas, and boost your marketing efforts.

OnCloudX uses the Google Maps API for accurate location results. To provide weather information, the Dark Sky API is utilized. For imagery Pixabay will populate a photo to provide a visual for the information given.
